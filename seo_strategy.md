# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public events listing (`/veranstaltungen`)
- Public newsletter signup pages (`/subscribe/:eventId`, `/subscribe/member/:memberId`)
- Public privacy page (`/datenschutz`)
- Shared HTML shell and server routing that affect public crawlability, metadata, social sharing, and AI crawler visibility

## Out of scope
- Authenticated admin area (`/`, `/events`, `/subscribers`, `/members`, `/qr-codes`, `/settings`)
- Member portal and account utility routes (`/mein-bereich`, `/passwort-reset`)
- Backend API routes except where they determine crawlability of public files such as `robots.txt` or `sitemap.xml`

## Target audience
- German-speaking visitors looking for Lions Club Meißner Land events and newsletter signup

## Primary keywords
- Lions Club Meißner Land Veranstaltungen
- Lions Club Meißner Land Newsletter
- Veranstaltungen Meißner Land
